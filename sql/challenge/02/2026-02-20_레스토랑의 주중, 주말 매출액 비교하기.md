1. **사용할 문법:**
   - CASE..WHEN...ELSE
   - SUM

## 정답 쿼리 (Solution)

```sql
-- 여기에 작성한 쿼리를 붙여넣으세요.
SELECT
CASE
WHEN day='Sat' THEN 'weekend'
WHEN day='Sun' THEN 'weekend'
ELSE 'weekday'
END AS week,
SUM(total_bill) as sales
FROM tips
GROUP BY week
ORDER BY sales DESC
```
